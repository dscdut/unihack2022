Manulife Danang International Marathon

The Danang International Marathon has been held in the namesake coastal city since 2013, offering four distances: Full Marathon, Half Marathon, 10km and 5km. The race was the first marathon in Vietnam to be certified by AIMS and become a Boston Marathon classifier. Check out more here.
